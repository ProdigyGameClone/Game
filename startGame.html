How to play?

When the game starts, you and a monster have 100hp, and you go first. Your character can choose a spell. After that, If you resolve a task correctly, you will hit the monster. Then it's the moster's turn. The game ends when you have got no hp. If the monster dies, you will move on to the next level and fight with a new monster.
